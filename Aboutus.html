WHAT WE DO

At TEAMUP, we specialize in providing engineering, procurement, and construction services for a wide range of industries, including oil and gas, petrochemical, mining, hydropower, energy, and renewable energies. Our team is experienced in international EPC projects, and we are committed to delivering high-quality engineering solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading EPC contractor in the North African region, especially in Tunisia, by providing innovative and sustainable engineering solutions that contribute to the economic and social development of the communities we work in. We strive to achieve this vision by building strong relationships with our clients, partners, based on mutual trust, respect, and integrity.

MISSION

Our mission at TEAMUP is to deliver high-quality EPC services that meet the highest international standards of safety, quality, and environmental sustainability.
We are committed to meeting our clients' needs and expectations by providing innovative, cost-effective, and sustainable solutions that enhance their business performance and contribute to their success.

Values

Our values define who we are and how we work. We operate with integrity, respect, and transparency in all our interactions with our clients.
We strive to be accountable for our actions, and we are committed to continuously improving our skills, knowledge, and processes to deliver the best possible outcomes for our clients.

QUALITY MANAGEMENT

At TEAMUP, we are dedicated to providing our clients with high-quality engineering solutions that meet the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental sustainability. To achieve this goal, we are in the process of establishing a comprehensive quality management system based on the ISO 9001:2015 standard. Our experienced team has a strong track record of working with international standards such as ISO and DIN.

We are committed to continuously improving our processes, training our staff, and optimizing our operations to ensure that our clients receive the best possible service. Through our focus on quality management, we strive to exceed our clients' expectations and deliver sustainable, value-added solutions that drive their success.
